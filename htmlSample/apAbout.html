In 2004 we started Arun Pipes as a small scale industry amidst of the huge competitors in the PVC pipe manufacturing industry

we started manufacturing pipes below 4 inches,Initially had tough time in the market matching the quality and price with the competitors product.But slowly we started finding the solutions that helped us to acheive them, By 2007 we started manufacturing 5 and 7 inch pipes which helped us to attract borewell customers as well..though we have faced tons of struggles we never compromised on the quality we delivered that created the better impression on our product among the customers.

Then we focussed on finding ways to reduce manufacturing costs,thereby reducing the pipes selling price.Later on we have acheived that by importing latest machineries.finally we manufactured good quality of pipes with minimal costs.We have occupied above 50% of the market.

In 2011 arun completed his studies in B.Sc (Electronics) at P.S.G college Coimbatore, joined the company,Being young and dynamic he took a worldwide tour to see the latest technologies used in PVC pipe manufacturing and imported latest machines,this has increased the quantity and quality of the PVC pipe production of the the company.

In 2012we followed the standards and received BAS ISO:9001 2008 certificate which added feather to our product.
